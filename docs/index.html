LETTER OF INTRODUCTION (MIND THE GAP DIGITAL ONBOARDERS & DEEL AMBASSADORS CDS GROUP)

This is to introduce with State Code as a member of the Mind The Gap Digital Onboarders & DEEL Ambassadors CDS Group in your state which was launched on Thursday, the 3rd day of June, 2021 by the Hon. Minister for Youths & SportsDevelopment,Mr.Sunday Dare.

WHAT IS A DIGITAL ONBOARDER & DEEL AMBASSADOR CDS?

The Digital Onboarders & DEEL Ambassadors CDS is a Special CDS Group with a goal to Train,Mentor and Support members of the CDS to;

  1. Acquire relevant digital skills based on the DEEL programme of the Hon.Minister and Ministry and
  2. Become a DEEL Ambassador by using the skills acquired to assist 5 Small Businesses to use Digital skills/tools to grow their businesses
  3. And train 100 Students in their host communities to use Digital skills and tools to become better students and Digital Citizens.
  4. Participate in the Annual #TakeTheDEEL Interstate Hackathon

This is a very unique CDS Group and it's very important to the Hon.Minister and Ministry.We would therefore appreciate every form of support given to the bearer of this letter towards a successful program implementation.

Thank you very much for your anticipated support.
